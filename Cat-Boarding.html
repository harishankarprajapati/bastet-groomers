How We Work?

Contact us

Reach out to us through our website or by phone to inquire about availability and to schedule a tour of our facility.

Reserve a spot

Once you have decided to book a stay for your cat, we will reserve a spot for them and provide you with all the necessary information to prepare for their arrival. first preference for annual membership holders

Drop off

On the day of arrival, bring your cat and any necessary items, complete the check-in process, and leave your furry friend in the care of our trained staff. we also provide cat taxis for pick-up & drop.

FAQ

Do you provide individual feeding schedules for each cat?
Yes, we do provide personalized feeding schedules for each cat based on their specific dietary needs.
Do you offer any kind of recreational activities for the cats?
Yes, we offer daily playtime sessions for our feline guests to ensure they stay active and entertained during their stay.
Can I bring my cat's bed, toys, and other items from home?
Yes, you are welcome to bring any items from home that will make your cat feel more comfortable and at ease during their stay.
What is your policy on administering medications to the cats?
Our staff is trained to administer any necessary medications, and we follow all instructions provided by the owner to ensure the health and well-being of your cat.
Is there a veterinary on-call in case of an emergency?
Yes, we have a veterinarian on call 24/7 for any medical emergencies that may arise during your cat's stay with us.
